My Favourite Hobby
Drawing

My hobby is drawing. Whenever I get free time I draw something. I like to draw many things like sceneries, sketches, paintings, modern art, etc. I also draw Madhubani Painting, Sohrai Painting, etc. I also draw my feelings. Drawing my emotions makes me feel light and happy. I like both modern art and ancient art. Drawing is a way to express our feelings on a piece of paper. Drawing needs 3 Ps which are Pratice, Presentation and most important Patience. Whenever I complete a drawing, It gives me so much pleasure. I love Drawing.
